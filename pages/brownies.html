Quick and Easy Brownies

Description

This easy brownie recipe takes about 30 minutes to make from start to finish for a chocolaty treat!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 4 eggs
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup walnut halves
